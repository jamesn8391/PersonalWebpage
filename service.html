Houston Food Bank:

I helped by assisting in packing home delivery kits and disaster boxed, particularly right after Hurricane Harvey in August and September of 2017. I also volunteered as part of my high school and donated goods. I helped to pack and organize said goods and distribute to the people that needed them. Prestonwood Animal Hospital

I volunteered here and helped assist with day-to-day operations including sanitizing, feeding, and overall making sure the animals were comfortable. Specifically, this included things like cleaning out litterboxes and ensuring that every animal had adequate water at all times, as well as generally checking their condition. This was done outside of my High School but was part of the Harris County Pets volunteering program. Of course this comes with the added benefit of being able to play with many cute cats and dogs.

KleinHack Hackathon

I volunteered as part of the first and second hackathons that my high school hosted in 2018 and 2019. I was an event organizer for both and was mostly involved in mentoring attendees that did not have much coding experience. Hackathons are a valuable learning opportunity for programmers of all experiences and helping to bring a hackathon to my high school and the school district is something that I was proud to be a part of. Unfortunately, I did not get the opportunity to help in 2020 because the event was canceled that year.
